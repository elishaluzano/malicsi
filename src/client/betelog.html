Founded on March 2017, The Betelog Team aims to provide an online platform to cater stakeholder's business problems.


 //ARSENAL

To keep up with the modern development in web apps, Betelog Devs utilize Angular Js, a JavaScript-based open-source front-end web application framework.

For front-end designing and experience, Betelog Team chose MaterializeCSS to aid in constructing attractive, consistent and functional web pages.

The Betelog team plays with MySql for data storing and loading. MySQL, if not the best, is one of the most popular Database Management Systems in the whole world.
